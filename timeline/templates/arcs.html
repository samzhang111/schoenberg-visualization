Provenance History for Rare Manuscripts

What kind of data are displayed?

The data shown here represent the manuscripts catalogued in the Schoenberg Database of Manuscripts. This database is an enormous,
publically-available repository of texts available prior to 1600. We offer a novel perspective on the relationships underlying these
data through their visual presentation, in addition to a glimpse of the manuscripts' journey throughout history.

What does this graph tell us?

A single path originates from a point and ends at another point. Such a path represents the exchange of a manuscript between two parties.
Multiple paths for a single manuscript indicate that this text has changed hands multiple times.

A blue line descending from a point represents the nubmer of exchanges for a particular manuscript. This quantity goes hand in hand with
the paths spanning the journey of the same manuscript.
